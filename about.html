About TotalCompCalc.com

TotalCompCalc.com is a free browser-based tool for comparing two job offers side by side. It estimates total compensation over a 1-year, 2-year, or 4-year horizon using base salary, bonus, equity, benefits, signing bonus, and expected annual raises.

Purpose of This Calculator

This tool exists to answer a straightforward question: which offer actually pays more over time? Nominal salary comparisons miss the impact of equity, bonuses, and raises. This calculator combines all components into a single total compensation estimate so you can compare offers on a consistent basis.

Who This Tool Is For

This calculator is useful for job seekers evaluating competing offers, employees reviewing compensation packages, and anyone who wants a structured estimate of total earnings before accepting a role.

How The Calculator Works

The calculator takes your inputs for each offer — base salary, target bonus percentage, bonus payout probability, signing bonus, annualized equity value, benefits value, and expected annual raise — and estimates total compensation year by year over your selected horizon. It does not model taxes, vesting schedules, or employer-specific payroll rules. All calculations run locally in your browser. No data is collected or stored.

About the Network
