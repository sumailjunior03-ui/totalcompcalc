When This Calculator Is Useful

This calculator is useful when you are comparing two job offers and want to estimate total compensation over time. It is built for job seekers who need a side-by-side view of salary, bonus, equity, benefits, and raises before making an offer decision.

What This Calculator Does

This calculator compares two job offers using the inputs you enter for base salary, bonus, equity, benefits, signing bonus, and expected annual raises. It estimates total compensation over a 1-year, 2-year, or 4-year horizon so you can compare offers on a consistent basis.

Calculation Methodology

The calculator estimates annual compensation for each offer by combining base salary, expected bonus, annualized equity value, annual benefits value, and any one-time signing bonus. For multi-year comparisons, it applies the expected annual raise to base salary for each future year, recalculates bonus from the updated salary, and sums the results across the selected horizon. This is an informational estimate only and does not model taxes, vesting schedules, cliffs, refresh grants, or actual employer payout rules.
