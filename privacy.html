Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Zero-Data Model

TotalCompCalc.com is a static, client-side tool. All compensation calculations run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No salary figures, offer details, or personal information are sent to any server, stored in any database, or shared with any third party by this site.

We do not operate user accounts, logins, or any form of session storage. When you close or refresh the page, all input data is discarded.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Salary amounts or compensation figures entered into the calculator
  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Employer names or job titles
  • Device identifiers, cookies set by this site, or persistent local storage
